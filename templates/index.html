Greenhouse Gas Emissions

The purpose of this visualization project was to analyze the change in global emissions between 2007-2017, to see if there was an effort to lower the output of carbon emissions. To perform this analysis, data from the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development(OECD) was collected. The OECD is an intergovernmental economic organisation with 36 member countries founded in 1961 to stimulate economic progress and world trade.

After assembling the dataset, Python, Javascript, Leaflet, and Flask were used to create the visualizations. Factors looked at include: percent change in emissions, greenhouse gas emissions per capita, and the country names associated with the OECD. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well a brief explanation and descriptions of trends witnessed.
